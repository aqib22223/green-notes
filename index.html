Our Story

Bridging Education and Sustainability

Founded by Albukhary International University students frustrated with paper waste in academia, Green Notes has grown into the leading platform for sustainable learning.

Our mission is to reduce the environmental impact of education while enhancing learning outcomes through innovative technology and community-driven solutions.

Our Journey

Milestones in Sustainable Education

Founded at Albukhary International University

January 2023

Green Notes was born from a dorm room project aiming to reduce paper waste in university courses. Our MVP saved 5,000 sheets in the first semester.

Seed Funding

June 2023

Raised $2.5M in seed funding from prominent edtech and sustainability investors to expand our team and technology.

Platform Launch
